Lakeside Oasis, a Virtual Real Estate Project

Oculus Rift • Unity • Javascript • Revit
Fall 2018, for the Virtual Reality course at the University of Illinois, with team VRchitects.

I had the fortune of designing a ludicrously oversized single-family home from scratch and converting the Revit project into a Unity experience. I furnished and textured much of the house with Unity assets. I developed the locomotion and scene-switch-based optimization.
